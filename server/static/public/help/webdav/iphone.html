WebDAV for iPhone

iPhone has several apps that can access Attachix

Tested with iPhone 3GS.

Air Sharing Pro

  1. Open Air Sharing Pro
  2. Click on +
  3. Click on WebDAV Server
  4. Enter "attachix.de" or the shared with you URL (something like ab4chQf.k.attachix.de)
  5. Enter "Attachix" as alias
  6. Enter your username (lowercase)
  7. Enter your password
  8. Click Save

ezShare Pro

  1. Open ezShare
  2. Click on Add a Server...
  3. Click on Other WebDAV
  4. Enter "Attachix" as a description
  5. Enter "attachix.de" or the shared with you URL (something like ab4chQf.k.attachix.de)
  6. Enter your username (lowercase) as user
  7. Enter your password
  8. Click on Save
